Early Childhood and Basketball

As young as first grade my love for basketball grew like no other! From the first day I touched a basketball, I knew it would help me get somewhere in life. Whether that meant to get me to see new places or prolong me in my education.

High School

As I got older, I chose the high school I felt would help me achieve what I was looking for. At that point, I knew I wanted to play basketball in college, and had so much support around me to do so. I was a district champion my sophomore year, all-district that year and my senior year, and an All-Star my last year. I signed at the end of the season to Trinidad State College in Trinidad, Colorado.

College Experience

I started my collegiate career at Trinidad State College. I spent a year there going to school, learning the game at the next level, and developing more of a sense of what I wanted out of my experience. From there I moved to Seattle, Washington, where I attended Edmonds College for my second year of eligibility. I truly loved the move and change of scenery. From the dry desert heat, to the beautiful green Pacific Northwest. My experience there shaped who I am now as a person, player, and student. Those first two years of college basketball taught me a lot about life and who I wanted to be.

Today

After all the stress and uncertainty, I am so proud to say I am where I am now. Little did I know taking all the risks I did would lead me here! VIU is now one of the homes I have engraved in my heart, and I am so proud to play for the coaches and teammates I play for. The journey is the dream!
