Where I'm From

I was born in Las Cruces, New Mexico, which is in the southern part of the United States. Born and raised there, I was itching to have my opportunity to start my life somewhere new. After 18 years I finally made my first move away from home, which landed me in Trinidad, Colorado. It was the first time I really was on my own, being in the state above my home one. This was the first stop of an incredible journey, as I only spent a year there. Shortly from there, I made the big 25 hour drive to the Pacific Northwest. I moved to Seattle, Washington to attend my second school, which somehow led me to the one I am at now. Another year passing and I made the move over to Nanaimo, BC, Canada. I am glad to say I have a little home in each of the places I have been to.

The journey back home for me.
